A "PO number" stands for "Purchase Order number." It is a unique identifier assigned to a specific purchase order within a business or organization.

A purchase order is a document issued by a buyer to a seller, indicating types, quantities, and agreed prices for products or services the seller will provide to the buyer. The PO number helps track and manage orders efficiently, especially in larger organizations where multiple orders may be processed simultaneously.

The PO number typically appears on various documents related to the transaction, including the purchase order itself, invoices, shipping documents, and receipts. It serves as a reference number for all parties involved in the purchasing process to easily identify and track the associated order.
