## ⏳ Structure & Timing

A typical HR stage lasts **15–30 minutes**, often after (or alongside) codes tests:

- Quick filtering: checking responses, culture fit, practical details.
- Some candidates report immediate scheduling of tech interviews afterward if HR call goes well.

## 💡 Tips to Stand Out

- Prepare your **concise self-intro**: highlight key skills, relevant experiences.
- Be ready to explain:
    - Why Odoo?
    - How your background fits the role.
- Prepare examples demonstrating soft skills:
    - Handling feedback, working in teams, solving conflict.
- Ensure clear, confident communication:
    - If writing is tested, be structured and use proper grammar.
- Be ready for:
    - Notice period, salary expectations.
    - Aptitude questions or reasoning puzzles.
